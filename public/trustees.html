Trustees & Wardens

The board meets in person on the first Saturday of November, at the Sussex lodge, and at no other time.

The trust is small. Several of its officers are related, by blood, marriage, or proximity. This is acknowledged.

The November Meeting

First Saturday, at the Sussex lodge, in person, since 1926.

The board meets once a year and at no other time. The meeting is held on the first Saturday of November, at the warden’s lodge in Sussex, in person. The agenda is always the same: read each warden’s survey, file it, sign the year’s charter renewal, take tea. The meeting has been held without interruption since 1926, save for the four meetings of 1939 to 1942, which were conducted by post.

Visitors and members of the public may not attend. Walkers passing the lodge on that Saturday are welcome to look in, and most years one or two do.
